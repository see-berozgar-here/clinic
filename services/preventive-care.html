💊 Preventive Health Checkups

Detect Health Risks Early - Invest in Your Wellness

Stay Healthy with Regular Checkups

Preventive health checkups are the best way to detect health problems early before they become serious. Our comprehensive screening packages assess your overall health and help you make informed lifestyle choices.

What's Included in Checkups:

  • ✓ Complete physical examination
  • ✓ Blood pressure and weight checks
  • ✓ Blood sugar level testing
  • ✓ Cholesterol and lipid profile
  • ✓ Thyroid function tests
  • ✓ Liver and kidney function tests
  • ✓ Complete blood count (CBC)
  • ✓ Urinalysis

Benefits of Preventive Care:

  • ✓ Early detection of diseases
  • ✓ Reduced treatment costs
  • ✓ Better quality of life
  • ✓ Increased life expectancy
  • ✓ Peace of mind
  • ✓ Prevention of complications
  • ✓ Personalized health guidance
  • ✓ Improved overall wellness

Recommended Checkup Frequency:

Annually: Healthy individuals with no risk factors | Twice Yearly: Those with chronic conditions | Quarterly: Elderly patients or those with multiple health issues
