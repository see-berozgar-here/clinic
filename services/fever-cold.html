Fever, Cold & Infection Management

We provide expert diagnosis and effective treatment for common infections, fever, cold, and respiratory conditions. Our goal is quick relief and proper care.

Conditions We Treat:

  • ✓ Common cold and cough
  • ✓ High fever management
  • ✓ Respiratory infections
  • ✓ Sore throat and tonsillitis
  • ✓ Flu and viral infections
  • ✓ Bronchitis and bronchiolitis
  • ✓ Sinusitis and nasal infections
  • ✓ Bacterial infections

Our Treatment Approach:

  • ✓ Accurate diagnosis and testing
  • ✓ Personalized treatment plans
  • ✓ Prescription antibiotics if needed
  • ✓ Symptomatic relief and comfort
  • ✓ Home care guidance
  • ✓ Prevention and immunity tips
  • ✓ Follow-up monitoring
  • ✓ Referral to specialists if needed

When to Visit?

Visit us if you have persistent fever, prolonged cough, difficulty breathing, severe throat pain, or any signs of infection. Early treatment prevents complications and speeds recovery.
