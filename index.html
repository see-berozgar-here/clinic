Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and treatments

01. How can I manage my diabetes effectively?

+

Effective diabetes management involves regular monitoring, personalized diet and exercise plans, prescribed medications, and lifestyle changes. Dr. Prasad provides comprehensive guidance and regular follow-ups to help you maintain healthy blood sugar levels and prevent complications.

02. Do you offer personalized treatment plans?

+

Yes, every patient receives a personalized care plan tailored to their specific health condition, age, lifestyle, and medical history. We believe in individualized treatment approach for the best outcomes.

03. Can I get tested for various health conditions at your clinic?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive diagnostic services including blood tests, ECG, and other advanced investigations. Our lab collection services make testing convenient, and all tests are conducted with advanced medical technology.

04. Is the clinic open for general medical consultations?

+

Absolutely! While we specialize in diabetes management, we provide comprehensive general medical services including treatment for fever, cold, infections, hypertension, thyroid disorders, and routine health checkups.

05. How often should I visit for follow-ups?

+

Follow-up frequency depends on your condition and treatment plan. Generally, diabetes patients should visit every 1-3 months for monitoring. Dr. Prasad will recommend the best schedule based on your specific health needs.

06. Do you offer vaccination services?

+

Yes, we provide adult vaccination services and preventive immunization programs. Vaccinations are an important part of preventive healthcare to protect you from various diseases.
