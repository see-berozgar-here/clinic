Protect Yourself with Vaccinations

Our comprehensive adult vaccination programs help protect you from preventable diseases. We provide expert guidance on recommended vaccines based on your age, health status, and travel plans.

Vaccines We Offer:

  • ✓ Influenza (Flu) vaccination
  • ✓ Pneumococcal vaccine
  • ✓ Tetanus and Diphtheria (Td/Tdap)
  • ✓ COVID-19 vaccination
  • ✓ Hepatitis A and B
  • ✓ Measles, Mumps, Rubella (MMR)
  • ✓ Varicella (Chickenpox)

Why Get Vaccinated?

  • ✓ Prevention of serious diseases
  • ✓ Community immunity protection
  • ✓ Reduced healthcare costs
  • ✓ Better health outcomes
  • ✓ Safe and effective protection
  • ✓ Prevents disease spread
  • ✓ Long-term immunity
  • ✓ WHO recommended programs

Expert consultation on vaccine recommendations, safe administration by trained professionals, vaccination records and certificates, adverse event monitoring, and follow-up guidance for booster shots.
