## 📞 How It Works (User Flow)

### **Patient Journey:**
```
1. Visit Website
   ↓
2. Choose Contact Method:
   - Call Now (📞)
   - WhatsApp (💬)
   - Inquiry Form (📝)
   - Forum Discussion
   ↓
3. Fill Out Form/Discussion
   ↓
4. Submit
   ↓
5. Doctor Receives Email
   ↓
6. Doctor Responds
```

### **Forum Submission Flow:**
```
1. Patient visits forum.html
2. Clicks "Start New Discussion"
3. Fills out detailed form:
   - Personal info (name, email, age)
   - Health category
   - Duration
   - Severity level
   - Detailed description
4. Clicks "Post Discussion & Submit to Doctor"
5. Form submits to Formspree
6. Doctor receives email with:
   - All patient information
   - Health concern details
   - Severity level (for prioritization)
7. Doctor responds via email/phone
```
